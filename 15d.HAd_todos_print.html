Hospital admissions

For the present analysis Ive been looking for the rate of hospital admissions among 211.367 patients from June 2013 to July 2018. In that period 21.235 hospital admissions happened, 2.019 were due to conditions sensitive to ambulatory care.

190.420 (90.0%) were never hospitalized and 20.947 (10.0%) were hospitalized at least once.

The linkage between the hospital admition dataset and the EMR dataset is not finished yet and most likely it will change the confidence intervals.

The unit of analysis was a Family Health Team (FHT) in a month with the exposure affecting the FHT. For every FHT we have the variables month, number of patients, number of consultations, number of adults, number of patients referred to secondary care, number of pregnant women, social development index, number of hospital admittions, number of ACSC and one variable for each group of ACSC.
